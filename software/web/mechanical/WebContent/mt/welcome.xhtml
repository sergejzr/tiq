Win the #{msg['competitionTitle']}



This is a performance based competition with 5 individual winners and 5 winning teams. You will be randomly assigned to one of 20 teams of annotators after completing one batch of 100 units successfully (i.e. getting the test example right once).

You will be shown a reference image of a face and asked to identify the same person within another set of ten images. A correctly identified face is worth 1 point.

The units will be processed in batches of 100. For quality control one of the tasks in a batch will be a test case; the position of the test case within the batch will be random. The test example will be shown after the batch is finished. If the test example was annotated correctly, you will receive the 100 points for the batch; if the test example was incorrectly annotated, you will receive no points for the finished batch and 20 points will be subtracted from the score instead.

Throughout the competition, there will be one hour long bonus events During bonus events, points for completed batches will be increased: points if the test case is annotated correctly points are subtracted if the test case is annotated incorrectly).

You will see your team's position and points as well as position and points of the three teams ranked below and above, as well as your share of the team's reward at all times. Similarly, you will see your individual points and position as well as position and points of the three players below and above. The top-10 team leaderboard and the top-10 individuals leaderboard will be revealed at the end of the competition.

All members of teams as well as individuals among the respective top-5 at the end of the competition will receive their reward afterwards via bonus payment.
